Cyberpunk Streetfighter

Unveiled at Motoverse 2025, the ALX is a radical reinterpretation of the Royal Enfield Guerrilla 450. A collaboration between Autologue Design and Dhone Automobiles, this build transforms the roadster into a futuristic streetfighter. It features sharp, angular body panels, exposed mechanical elements, and a "Johnny Silverhand" inspired aesthetic. With a custom machined subframe and carbon fiber overlays, the ALX looks like a weapon engineered for tomorrow's streets.
